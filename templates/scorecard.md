# B2B Standards Scorecard

One file, two jobs: hold your repeatable case study (Standard 1), and hold a dated weekly row of your numbers against the other five standards. This is the memory your weekly review reads and appends to.

How to use it:

- Rough honest numbers beat precise flattering ones. "Unknown" is an answer, and usually a finding.
- Append, never overwrite. New rows go on top. The history is what lets you debug week over week.
- Your contract band sets your bars: Big ($100k+) needs 3 meetings/week and 90-day cycles. Moderate ($25k to $99k) needs 10 and 30. Small (sub-$25k) needs 15 and 14.
